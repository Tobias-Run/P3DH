0010EU KM1 Key metrics template As at 31 December 2025, AS Inbank’s total own funds amounted to EUR 197 million (31 December 2024: EUR 179 million). The Total Capital ratio stood at 18.81% (31 December 2024: 18.90%).During 2024, the Common Equity Tier 1 (CET1), Tier 1 and Total Capital increased due to capital issuances and earned profit. In April 2024, AS Inbank raised EUR 2.34 million of additional Tier 1 capital. In August 2024, the institution raised EUR 10 million of additional capital from shareholders. In December 2024, AS Inbank executed a synthetic securitisation transaction qualifying for Significant Risk Transfer (SRT), which reduced risk-weighted exposure amounts (RWEA). As a result of capital raises, retained earnings and the securitisation transaction, capital ratios improved compared to 31 December 2023.In January 2025, AS Inbank called EUR 3.15 million of Tier 1 instruments and EUR 8 million of Tier 2 instruments, which resulted in a temporary reduction in own funds during the first half of 2025. In the second half of 2025, the institution strengthened its capital base by issuing EUR 8 million of Tier 2 capital in October 2025 and EUR 5 million of new CET1 capital in December 2025.The Total Risk Exposure Amount (RWEA) amounted to EUR 1,045 million as at 31 December 2025 (31 December 2024: EUR 947 million). The increase during 2025 was primarily driven by growth in retail lending exposures, partly offset by the continued effect of the synthetic securitisation transaction executed in 2024. The leverage ratio stood at 10.71% (31 December 2024: 10.53%), well above the regulatory minimum requirement of 3%. The increase reflects strengthened Tier 1 capital, partially offset by growth in total exposures.As at 31 December 2025, the Liquidity Coverage Ratio (LCR) stood at 888% (31 December 2024: 524%), remaining significantly above the regulatory minimum requirement of 100%. The Net Stable Funding Ratio (NSFR) stood at 135.74% (31 December 2024: 120.14%), supported by a stable retail deposit base and balanced funding structure.
