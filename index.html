EBA Pillar 3 — Bank Disclosure Viewer

An independent, open research pipeline that turns the EU banks' public Pillar 3 regulatory disclosures into readable, browsable templates.

▶ Open the viewer View source on GitHub

What is this?

Large EU/EEA banks must publicly disclose prudential (“Pillar 3”) information — capital, risk, liquidity. The EBA centralises these on the Pillar 3 Data Hub as machine-readable XBRL-CSV. This project ingests those filings, resolves the DPM data-point dictionary, and reconstructs each bank's official templates (KM1, CCR1, …) with full row/column labels — viewable right in your browser, no install.

The viewer loads the processed CSVs live. It currently shows a sample of institutions; the full catalog covers 489 institutions / 4,278 filings.
